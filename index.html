Festivals & Celebrations

Gaur Maria Dance

A powerful ceremonial dance of the Gond tribe, performed during harvest festivals to honor deities, ancestors, and the rhythm of nature.

Gondi Celebrations

Vibrant community gatherings marked by folk songs, collective drumming, storytelling, and ritual dances passed down through generations.

Angami Naga Festival

A colorful celebration of the Angami Naga tribe showcasing warrior dances, traditional attire, harvest rituals, and ancestral pride.

Drum & Ritual Festivals

Sacred drumming ceremonies where rhythm, trance, and spiritual practices unite communities during important seasonal and religious events.

Warli Harvest Festival

A joyous harvest celebration expressed through iconic Warli paintings, circular dances, and symbolic storytelling of rural life.

Sohrai Festival

Celebrated by tribal communities of Jharkhand, Sohrai honors cattle, agriculture, and fertility through wall art and folk traditions.

Pithora Festival

A sacred ritual of the Bhil tribe where Pithora paintings are created to seek prosperity, protection, and blessings from local deities.

Khasi Cultural Festival

A celebration of Khasi tribal heritage highlighting matrilineal customs, nature worship, traditional music, and bamboo craftsmanship.
